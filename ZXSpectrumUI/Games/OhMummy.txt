Oh Mummy!
---------


The ancient magic of the Pharoes of Egypt, await those who would dare
desicrate their tombs, disturbing their slumber. Hoards of riches are to
be found, if you have the courage to face the guardians of the pyramids!!.

A = Move Up
Z = Move Down
K = Move Left
L = Move Right

Or redefine keys to use joystick.
